Lentil Soup

Ingredients

  • 200g Canned Lentils
  • 300g Potatoes
  • 100g Carrots
  • 100g Celery Root
  • 100g Leek
  • 500ml Beef Broth
  • 500ml Vegetable Broth
  • 1T Butter
  • 3T Balsamic Vinegar
  • Fresh Parsley

Preparation

  1. Cut Carrots, Celery, Leek and Potatoes in small Cubes. Not larger than 5mm.
  2. Wash the Lentils and put them in a Pot with the Vegetable Broth on low to medium heat for 30min.
  3. In a second bigger Pot, melt Butter and saut√© the Vegetables and deglaze with Beef Broth.
  4. Let the Vegetable Beef Soup simmer for 5min.
  5. Put the Lentil Soup into the Vegetable Soup.
  6. Taste and season it with Balsamic Vinegar.
  7. Garnish with Parsley.
