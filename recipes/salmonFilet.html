Salmon Fillet

with Potato Chips

Ingredients

  • 500g Salmon Fillet
  • 500g Potatoes
  • 1 Lemon
  • 1T Parika Powder
  • All purpose Flour
  • 1t Salt
  • 1t Pepper
  • 3T Olive Oil
  • 1T Dryed and cut Rosemary
  • 30g Butter

Preparation

  1. Preheat Oven to 180Â°C.
  2. Wash Potatoes and cut them in thicker Chips. Around 5mm width.
  3. Put the Chips into a bowl with a Lid.
  4. Put Salt, Pepper, Paprika Powder, Olive Oil and Rosemary into the Bowl. Close it with the Lid and shake well.
  5. Place the marinated Chips on a prepared Oven Grate with Baking Paper on it.
  6. Give it into the Oven for 20min.
  7. Preheat a coated Pan on medium heat.
  8. Press the Lemons over the Salmon.
  9. Season it with Salt and Pepper.
  10. Put the underside of the Salmon into the Flour, for a nice Crust.
  11. Put the Butter into the pan and let it melt.
  12. Cook the Salmon till it's golden brown.
  13. Everything should be ready at the same time.
